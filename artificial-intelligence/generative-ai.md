# Generative AI

## Overview

Generative AI is a branch of Artificial Intelligence that creates new content such as text, images, music, videos, and computer code. Instead of only analyzing existing data, Generative AI learns patterns from large datasets and generates new content that is similar to what it has learned.

Popular AI tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude are examples of Generative AI applications.

---

## Why Learn Generative AI?

- Creates content automatically
- Improves productivity
- Assists in software development
- Enhances creativity
- Powers modern AI applications

---

## How Generative AI Works

```text
Training Data
      │
      ▼
Train AI Model
      │
      ▼
Learn Patterns
      │
      ▼
User Prompt
      │
      ▼
Generate Content
```

## Best Practices

- Write clear and specific prompts.
- Verify AI-generated content for accuracy.
- Protect sensitive and confidential data.
- Use AI as a learning and productivity tool.
- Review generated content before publishing.
