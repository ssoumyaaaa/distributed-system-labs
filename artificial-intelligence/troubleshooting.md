# Troubleshooting and Common Mistakes

## Overview

When learning Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, beginners often encounter common challenges. Understanding these mistakes and knowing how to resolve them will help you build better models and improve your learning experience.

---

## Why Learn Troubleshooting?

- Identify common AI and ML mistakes
- Improve model performance
- Save development time
- Build reliable AI solutions
- Strengthen problem-solving skills

---

## Common Issues

### Poor Quality Data

**Problem:**
The model produces inaccurate predictions.

**Solution:**

- Remove incorrect or duplicate data.
- Handle missing values.
- Use clean and consistent datasets.

---

### Overfitting

**Problem:**
The model performs well on training data but poorly on new data.

**Solution:**

- Use more training data.
- Simplify the model.
- Test using unseen data.

---

### Underfitting

**Problem:**
The model cannot learn the patterns in the data.

**Solution:**

- Train the model longer.
- Use a more suitable algorithm.
- Improve the quality of training data.

---

### Choosing the Wrong Model

**Problem:**
The selected algorithm does not fit the problem.

**Solution:**

- Understand the problem first.
- Start with simple models.
- Compare different algorithms.

---

### Unrealistic Expectations

**Problem:**
Expecting AI to always provide perfect answers.

**Solution:**

- Remember that AI predictions are based on data.
- Verify important results.
- Continue improving the model over time.

---

## Best Practices

- Start with simple problems.
- Use clean and reliable data.
- Test models with unseen data.
- Learn from mistakes and improve gradually.
- Keep practicing with small projects.

---

## Summary

Troubleshooting is an essential part of learning AI and Machine Learning. By understanding common mistakes such as poor-quality data, overfitting, underfitting, and incorrect model selection, you can build more accurate and reliable AI solutions.
