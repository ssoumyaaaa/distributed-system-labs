# Introduction

## Overview

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) are transforming the way computers solve problems. From voice assistants and recommendation systems to self-driving cars and medical diagnosis, AI is becoming a part of everyday life.

This guide is designed for beginners who want to understand AI and ML using simple explanations, real-world examples, diagrams, and hands-on exercises.

---

## Why Learn AI and Machine Learning?

- One of the fastest-growing technology fields
- Used across healthcare, finance, retail, education, and many other industries
- Helps automate repetitive tasks
- Improves decision-making using data
- Opens career opportunities in software development and data science

---

## AI and ML at a Glance

```text
Artificial Intelligence (AI)
          │
          ▼
Machine Learning (ML)
          │
          ▼
Deep Learning (DL)
```

- **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** enables machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.
- **Machine Learning (ML)** is a subset of AI where systems learn from data instead of being explicitly programmed.
- **Deep Learning (DL)** is a specialized branch of ML that uses neural networks to solve complex problems.

## Summary
