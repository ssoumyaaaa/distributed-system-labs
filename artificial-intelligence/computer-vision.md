# Computer Vision

## Overview

Computer Vision is a branch of Artificial Intelligence that enables computers to understand and analyze images and videos. It allows machines to recognize objects, identify faces, detect text, and make decisions based on visual information.

Computer Vision combines image processing, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning to solve real-world problems.

---

## Why Learn Computer Vision?

- Enables computers to understand images
- Automates image and video analysis
- Improves accuracy in object detection
- Powers smart cameras and autonomous systems
- Supports healthcare and security applications

## Common Computer Vision Tasks

### Image Classification

Identifies the main object in an image.

Examples:

- Cat or Dog
- Car or Bike
- Apple or Orange

---

### Object Detection

Detects and locates multiple objects within an image.

Examples:

- Detect people in CCTV footage
- Identify vehicles on a road
- Count products on a shelf

---

### Face Recognition

Recognizes or verifies a person's identity using facial features.

Examples:

- Smartphone Face Unlock
- Office Attendance Systems
- Airport Security

---

### Optical Character Recognition (OCR)

Extracts text from images or scanned documents.

Examples:

- Scan invoices
- Read vehicle number plates
- Digitize printed documents

- Use high-quality images for training.
- Collect diverse datasets for better accuracy.
