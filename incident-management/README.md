# Incident Management

Incident Management is the process of identifying, responding to, resolving, and recovering from unexpected service disruptions while minimizing business impact.

This module introduces the concepts, processes, and best practices followed by Technology Support and Application Support teams to handle production incidents efficiently.

---

## 📚 Topics Covered

- Incident Management Overview
- Incident Lifecycle
- Priority vs Severity
- Incident Communication
- Escalation Matrix
- Major Incident Management (MIM)
- Root Cause Analysis (RCA)
- Post-Incident Review (PIR)
- Best Practices
- Interview Questions

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

After completing this module, you will be able to:

- Understand the Incident Management process
- Differentiate between incidents, problems, and changes
- Classify incidents using priority and severity
- Communicate effectively during production incidents
- Follow escalation procedures
- Participate in Major Incident Management
- Perform Root Cause Analysis (RCA)
- Conduct Post-Incident Reviews (PIR)
- Apply industry best practices to minimize service disruptions
